About Me

My professional career spans over 25 years working for two Fortune 500 companies, Electrolux subsidiary Dometic Corporation and Travelers Property Insurance, in the capacity of administrative assistant, office manager and purchasing agent; and commercial and residential property adjuster, respectively. In addition, I served as Principal Real Estate Broker/part-owner of an Indiana Real Estate Brokerage company.

My belief in being proactive as well as my intuative nature for problem-solving has led to my pursute in studying computer technology, specifically front-end web development. I have a B.A. in Psychology from Indiana University and a member of the National Honors Society in Psychology (Psi Chi). I have also studied computer science and am currently enrolled in Northwestern University's Professional Studies Computer Coding Bootcamp.
